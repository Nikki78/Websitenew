Heiltsuk Nation Wildlife

Mother Nature is our Healer and Provider

Heiltsuk unceded territory encompasses 35,553 square kilometers. Within this vast territory there is vast amount of beautiful wildlife to see. Alot of this wildlife the Heiltsuk still rely on for sustenance, and have since time immemorial. Sadly alot of the seafood supply can and has been ruined by oil spills. The most recent been the Nathan E.Stewart, which was a deisel spill that occured in 2016. This ruined a lot of our traditional seafood, as it happened right on a traditional harvesting site. Sadly it is ships like this that have made a highway of our traditional sites. It has put a a wrench in our way of living,but as well our being,because it is part of who we are as Heiltsuk people.

Heiltsuk Nation is part of the largest coastal temperate rainforest of the world. It contains the largest old growth forest in the world.

Some of the Wildlife of Heiltsuk Nation

There is plenty of beautiful wildlife to be seen, but this is just a few of mother nature's best to watch out for in Heiltsuk territory. If you are out on a boat ride it is common to see many of these beautiful beings. We do not treat them as separate but it is part of who we are as Heiltsuk people. We are taught to respect them as beings with spirit. But as well we understand that some of them give their lives for us to survive, and we always thank them after a hunt or fishing season. This is what was taught by our elders. And to only take what we need.
